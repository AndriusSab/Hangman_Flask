Welcome to Hangman Game!

Hangman is a word guessing game where you have to guess the letters of a secret word. You have a limited number of attempts (10) to guess the word correctly before the hanging man is completed.

Game Rules:
  • A random word is chosen as the secret word.
  • You can guess one letter at a time.
  • If the guessed letter is in the secret word, it will be revealed in the correct positions.
  • If the guessed letter is not in the secret word, part of the hanging man is drawn.
  • You can continue guessing letters until you either guess the entire word or the hanging man is completely drawn.

Have fun playing Hangman!
